Firstly, I received the access code late on October 13th, which led to delays in my course registration. I had to wait for the results of my placement tests, and as a result, I was only able to finalize my course registration between October 20th and 23rd. Furthermore, I have faced some psychological and health-related difficulties that have prevented me from attending some midterm exams for the courses I am currently enrolled in. After carefully assessing my situation, I believe that deferring this semester is the most suitable course of action for me. This decision will allow me to focus on improving my mental and physical health and to better prepare myself for the challenges of the upcoming semester. It is my sincere hope to overcome the circumstances that affected me this semester, so I can perform to the best of my ability and contribute positively to this esteemed university, of which I am proud to be a member. Thank you in advance for your understanding and support. I am confident that your priority is the well-being and success of all students in this great institution. مرفوض
