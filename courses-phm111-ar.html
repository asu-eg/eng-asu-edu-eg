English Describtion

Fundamental mathematical concepts required for engineering studies. The course covers algebra, trigonometry, functions, limits, differentiation, and basic integration techniques, with applications relevant to engineering problems and analytical thinking.
Arabic Describtion

Fundamental mathematical concepts required for engineering studies. The course covers algebra, trigonometry, functions, limits, differentiation, and basic integration techniques, with applications relevant to engineering problems and analytical thinking.
Course Aims

This course aims to provide students with essential mathematical tools and techniques necessary for understanding and solving basic engineering and scientific problems.
