English Describtion

Economic principles applied to engineering decision-making. Topics include time value of money, cost analysis, interest rates, depreciation, investment evaluation methods, and economic comparison of engineering alternatives.
Arabic Describtion

Economic principles applied to engineering decision-making. Topics include time value of money, cost analysis, interest rates, depreciation, investment evaluation methods, and economic comparison of engineering alternatives.
Course Aims

This course aims to equip students with economic analysis tools needed to evaluate engineering projects and make informed financial and investment decisions.
