English Description

Advanced mathematical concepts for engineering applications. The course covers techniques of integration, sequences and series, multivariable calculus, partial differentiation, multiple integrals, and an introduction to differential equations with practical engineering applications.
Arabic Description

Advanced mathematical concepts for engineering applications. The course covers techniques of integration, sequences and series, multivariable calculus, partial differentiation, multiple integrals, and an introduction to differential equations with practical engineering applications.
Course Aims

This course aims to develop students' ability to apply advanced mathematical methods in solving engineering problems and to enhance their analytical and problem-solving skills.
